# Ready-to-Use AI Prompts for Spar Card Generation

**Project:** Spar Card Game
**Theme:** Afro-Heritage
**Total Cards:** 35
**Format:** 512x768px PNG

---

## How to Use These Prompts

1. **Copy the entire prompt** for the card you want to generate
2. **Paste into Midjourney** (recommended) or DALL-E 3
3. **For Midjourney:** Add parameters: `--ar 2:3 --v 6 --quality 2 --style raw`
4. **Generate 4 variations** and select the best
5. **Note the seed number** for consistency
6. **Post-process** as described in TASK-022-AI-GENERATION-PLAN.md
