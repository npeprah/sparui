## Third-Party Modifications

Any third-party sounds that have been modified will include:
1. Original source and license
2. Detailed list of modifications (trim, normalize, EQ, layering, etc.)
3. Confirmation that license allows modifications
4. Attribution to original creator (if required)

**Common Modifications Applied:**
- Trimming to appropriate duration
- Volume normalization to -12 LUFS (SFX) or -14 LUFS (music)
- Fade in/fade out application
- EQ adjustments for clarity
- Reverb or spatial effects
- Layering multiple sounds
- Format conversion (WAV → OGG/MP3)
