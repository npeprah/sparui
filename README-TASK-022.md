## Key Design Principles

### Afro-Heritage Theme
- Warm cream backgrounds (#FFF5E6, #FFE8D6, #FFF9F0)
- Kente cloth patterns (subtle, 10-20% opacity)
- Gold metallic borders (#FFD700)
- Cultural authenticity, no stereotypes

### Suit Colors
- **Hearts:** Fire Red #FF4500 (passion)
- **Clubs:** Dark Green #2F4F2F (strength)
- **Diamonds:** Fire Red #FF4500 (prosperity)
- **Spades:** Near Black #1C1C1C (wisdom)

### Arcade Energy
- Bold, vibrant colors (never dim)
- High contrast, cel-shaded style
- Large suit symbols (160-192px)
- Neon glows and metallic accents
- Energetic, NOT subtle
