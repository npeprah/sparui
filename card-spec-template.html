Card Specification Templates

Exact measurements for AI generation reference - Use these as visual guides

Number Card Template

(6-10 of all suits)

Key Measurements

Card Dimensions: 512px × 768px (2:3 ratio)

Border Radius: 24px (rounded corners)

Border Width: 4px solid gold (#FFD700)

Corner Value: 40px (Orbitron Black/900)

Corner Suit: 28px suit symbol

Corner Position: 20-30px from edges

Center Symbol: 160px (60% of card height)

Pattern Opacity: 10% (subtle background)

Design Notes

  • Suit symbol should DOMINATE the card - make it LARGE
  • Kente pattern should be visible but subtle (background texture only)
  • Corner indicators readable even at thumbnail size
  • Gold border must be prominent and metallic
  • Warm cream background, NOT pure white

Face Card Template

(Jack, Queen, King of all suits)

Key Measurements

Portrait Area: 280px × 460px (60% of card)

Title Font: 48px Bebas Neue (KING/QUEEN/JACK)

Character Size: ~180px diameter (head/shoulders)

Rank Label: 18px Barlow Semi-Bold

Pattern Opacity: 20% (more visible than number cards)

Corner Indicators: Same as number cards (40px/28px)

Character Design Notes

  • Jack: Young warrior (20s), athletic, energetic
  • Queen: Regal leader (30s), elegant, commanding
  • King: Wise elder (40s+), authoritative, powerful
  • Modern African fashion with geometric patterns
  • Direct eye contact, frontal pose
  • Comic book/arcade style, NOT photorealistic
  • Bold black outlines (4px), cel-shaded rendering
  • Suit color in clothing, gold accents on regalia

Ace Card Template (Premium)

(Aces of Hearts, Clubs, Diamonds - NO Spades Ace)

Premium Measurements

Border Width: 6px (thicker than other cards)

Border Style: Gradient #FFD700 → #D4AF37

Corner Value: 48px (larger than other cards)

Corner Suit: 32px (larger than other cards)

Center Symbol: 192px (70% of card, MASSIVE)

Pattern Opacity: 15% (ornate, more visible)

Glow Effect: 12px blur @ 70% opacity

Art Deco Corners: Gold geometric flourishes

Premium Design Notes

  • MOST IMPRESSIVE card in the deck - go BIG
  • Symbol should have gradient mesh (red → orange → gold)
  • Metallic sheen with reflective highlights
  • Enhanced neon glow (stronger than other cards)
  • Slight 3D depth effect with shadow
  • Art Deco geometric patterns in corners
  • More ornate Kente pattern than other cards
  • MAXIMUM visual impact - show-stopping design

Quick Reference Checklist

Before You Start Generating

  • ✅ Card dimensions: 512×768px (2:3 aspect ratio)
  • ✅ Background: Warm cream gradient (NOT pure white)
  • ✅ Border: 4px gold #FFD700 (6px for aces)
  • ✅ Corners: 24px radius for rounded edges
  • ✅ Suit symbol: LARGE and centered (160px for numbers, 192px for aces)
  • ✅ Corner indicators: 40px value + 28px suit (48px/32px for aces)
  • ✅ Kente pattern: Subtle at 10-20% opacity
  • ✅ Style: Bold arcade, cel-shaded, NOT photorealistic
  • ✅ Colors: Match suit palette exactly
  • ✅ Energy: Vibrant and bold, NOT subtle or corporate

After AI Generation

  • ✅ Resize to EXACTLY 512×768px
  • ✅ Add/fix corner indicators if needed
  • ✅ Enhance Kente pattern if too subtle
  • ✅ Check gold border is visible
  • ✅ Adjust colors to match theme
  • ✅ Export as PNG-24 with transparency
  • ✅ Compress to <100KB with TinyPNG
  • ✅ Rename properly ([suit]_[value].png)
  • ✅ Save to correct folder
